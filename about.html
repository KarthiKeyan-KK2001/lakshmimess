About Us

Welcome to Lakshmi Mess, a premier vegetarian hang-out spot in Thuraiyur. We bring you a wide range of authentic South Indian delicacies – freshly prepared, quick to serve, and truly satisfying.

We pledge to our customers a flavour of home, uncompromising quality, and culinary creations crafted with passion. The food has remained the same. The original recipes are closely guarded and followed.

The humble roots have never been forgotten. This is food from the heart – cooked with joy, served with love, and kept simple. Just the way it’s meant to be – more than just a meal – it’s warmth of home, the joy of sharing, and the simplicity of love on a plate.

"Food from the heart — cooked with joy, served with love and kept simple."
